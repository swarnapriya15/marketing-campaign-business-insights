# Marketing Campaign Business Insights Report (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a marketing campaign dataset to understand campaign performance and identify the most effective marketing channels based on Return on Investment (ROI).

Instead of building a machine learning model, the focus is on generating business insights through data cleaning, visualization, and analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze marketing campaign performance and answer the question:

**Which marketing channels should receive more budget based on ROI?**

---

## 📂 Dataset

Marketing Campaign Dataset

The dataset contains information such as:

- Campaign Type
- Marketing Channel
- Target Audience
- Customer Segment
- Impressions
- Clicks
- Leads
- Conversions
- Acquisition Cost
- Revenue
- ROI
- Engagement Score

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Marketing channels show significant differences in ROI and revenue generation.
- Higher customer engagement is associated with increased conversions and revenue.
- The marketing funnel reveals customer drop-off from impressions to conversions.
- Acquisition costs vary across channels, highlighting opportunities to optimize spending.
- ROI analysis helps identify channels that deserve higher marketing budgets.

---

## 📈 Recommendations

- Allocate more budget to high-ROI channels.
- Optimize channels with high acquisition costs and lower conversions.
- Improve audience targeting and campaign content.
- Continuously monitor campaign KPIs.
